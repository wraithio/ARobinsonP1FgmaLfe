I ran a marathon last summer in San Francisco, and I think the toughest challenge was dealing with the time and weather. I trained for about six months in the afternoons under sunny Sacramento skies, but the marathon started at 5 a.m. on a cold Bay Area morning. I was well-prepared overall, though I’m not exactly thrilled with my time. Still, I’ve definitely caught the running bug and am already gearing up for a couple more races to finish off the year strong.

Even though I moved on from it, I know I always have those skills to fall back on if I ever need to.
