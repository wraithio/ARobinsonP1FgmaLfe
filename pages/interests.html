Sports

I love spending time watching and playing sports with my friends. My favorite sport to watch is basketball, and I'm a huge fan of the Sacramento Kings. I'm especially excited for this season because the Kings have a new addition, DeMar DeRozan, and I can't wait to see how he performs with the team. Besides basketball, I also enjoy watching football, and my favorite team is the Raiders. Whether it's basketball or football, cheering on my teams with friends always makes it more fun!

Weight Lifting

I take a lot of pride in going to the gym and lifting weights to improve my fitness. I try to make it to the gym multiple times a week, and my favorite lifts are chest flys and lat pull downs. I bench around 245 pounds, with a 205-pound working weight, and I can squat about 315 pounds, although I admit my legs still need some work. Recently, I've been getting into calisthenics, so I've started incorporating more body-weight exercises into my routine. It's been harder to find time for the gym lately, but I do my best to stick with it.

Clothes Design

I love designing clothes and have a business where I create clothing and other products for clothing brands and local businesses. I use both embroidery and graphic design to bring a wide range of products to life. It's a great creative outlet for me, and I really enjoy the process of turning ideas into something tangible that people can wear and appreciate.
